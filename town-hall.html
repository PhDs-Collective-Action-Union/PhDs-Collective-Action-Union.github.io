Hi everyone! We are happy to announce our hybrid Town Hall, which will take place Friday November 18th at 6pm in Room 3051 in the Arts Block at Trinity College. If you are planning on attending in person, we ask that you reserve a spot here using Eventbrite. This is simply so that we don’t have to turn people away if we are over capacity.

You can also join remotely on Zoom at https://ucsd.zoom.us/j/91741793037. You can add this to your calendar with this link.

The main event of the night will be the presentation of the near-final draft of the Fair Researcher Agreement for discussion. We will also present an update of our work so far, and our plan for the next 4 months.

The final version of the Fair Researcher Agreement will be sent to Simon Harris and all other notable politicians, provosts and SFI/IRC officials, and will therefore be a clear declaration of our position. It would be great to have as many people there as possible.https://www.eventbrite.com/e/pcau-town-hall-tickets-465659218257
