Jefferey Sardinia - President

Hello! I am Jeffrey (or Siothrún in Irish) Sardina, President of the PCAU. I am a full-time PhD researcher investigating knowledge graphs and machine learning at Trinity College Dublin. I ran for President on the platform of representing all PhD and postgraduate researchers in the demand for fair pay, equal treatment, and the breakdown of artificial barriers faced by non-EU and other marginalised PhDs. You are always welcome to reach out to me in either Irish or English.

Role:

In charge of general Union management, and also acts as a spokesperson in negotiations. The President shall chair all meetings; in case of their absence, the Vice President shall chair.
